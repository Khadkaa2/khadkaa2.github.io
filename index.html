About Us

We are a FTC team based out of Hebron High School in Carrollton, Texas. We were established in 2023. As a team, we strive to create a reliable robot through design techniques, organized code, and teamwork because we believe success comes as a result of collaboration.

>Here at Hebron, we build more than "just a robot". We build a community.

Despite consisting of two teams, our program fosters collaboration between all members through suggestions, advice, and shared resources. We may be two separate teams, but we still come from the same home—Hebron.

Every mistake we make is followed by growth as we continuously work to overcome difficulties in programming, design, and development. Behind each aspect of our robots lies the perseverance of our dedicated students.

Our program offers students with the space and tools needed to truly develop the skills required to step into the engineering industry. By teaching our members to learn various skills—including but not limited to CAD, design principles, programming through Java, and the assembly of parts—we have set our students up for their future in the workforce.
